DATE_TRUNCedit

Rounds down a date to the closest interval. Intervals can be expressed using the timespan literal syntax.

FROM employees
| EVAL year_hired = DATE_TRUNC(1 year, hire_date)
| STATS count(emp_no) BY year_hired
| SORT year_hired
